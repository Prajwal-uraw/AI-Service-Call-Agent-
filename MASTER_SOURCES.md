## How to Cite

### In Content:
```
According to ServiceTitan's 2023 Industry Report, 23% of HVAC calls go to voicemail, costing the average company $47,000 annually in lost revenue.
```

### Inline Citation:
```
23% of HVAC calls go to voicemail [1], costing companies an average of $47,000 per year [2].
```

### Sources Section:
```
## Sources & References

[1] ServiceTitan. (2023). "Home Services Industry Benchmarks Report"
[2] Calculated from ServiceTitan industry data and booking rate analysis
```
